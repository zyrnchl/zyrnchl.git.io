Sunset Pages – Online Bookstore

HTML · CSS · JavaScript

Sunset Pages is a warm and visually immersive online bookstore website themed around the beauty of sunsets and the joy of reading. The site features a stunning hero section with a curated gallery of sunset photography and cozy bookstore interiors, bringing to life the tagline "Where stories meet the sunset and imagination never ends."

Key features include an interactive 5-star rating system where users can click to rate and receive instant feedback, two themed image gallery sections with inspirational quotes, and a fully responsive layout built for a seamless experience across all devices.
