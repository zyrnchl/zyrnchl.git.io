SUNSET PAGES ONLINE BOOKSTORE

HTML · CSS · JavaScript

Sunset Pages is a visually immersive online bookstore inspired by the beauty of sunsets and the joy of reading. The platform combines a warm, sunset-themed design with curated galleries of photography and cozy bookstore interiors. It features interactive elements such as a 5-star rating system and genre-based book discovery, all built with responsive design principles to ensure a smooth experience across devices.
